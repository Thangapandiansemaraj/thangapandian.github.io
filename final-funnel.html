Crafted an insight-led leadership feature showcasing how Pan Weber’s transformed bread distribution through operational precision, freshness-focused logistics, and customer-centric supply chain management.

Overview

Strategy

Built around the core pillars of Freshness, Operational Precision, and Customer Reliability to demonstrate how the company balances centralized planning with localized market responsiveness.

Execution

Results
